# Research brief shape

The synthesized output. Lead with what's useful; don't dump.

```
# {TARGET} — Research Brief
_Researched {date}. Sources at bottom._

## Say-this-in-the-interview (top)
- {the 3-5 sharpest, most current things to know/say}

## What they do
{core business, how they make money, scale}

## Recent strategy & news (last ~12mo)
- {move + why it matters} [source]

## Competitive position
- Strong: ... · Exposed: ...

## Where they're investing
- ...

## (Market only) size / growth / players / trends

## Sources
- {cited links}
```

Keep verified facts separate from your analysis. Don't assert anything the research didn't support.

# Job Description: Business Analyst — Vanguard Strategy Group

## Location
Toronto, ON (Hybrid)

## About the Role
Vanguard Strategy Group is a boutique operations and growth advisory firm. We help mid-market clients navigate complex transformations, supply chain re-engineering, and digital strategy. As a Business Analyst, you will work directly with consultants and clients to gather data, build quantitative models, structure business problems, and present recommendations.

## Core Responsibilities
- **Quantitative Modeling**: Build bottoms-up demand, cost-sizing, and operational throughput models in Excel and Tableau.
- **Problem Structuring**: Use MECE issue trees and business frameworks to break down ambiguous client problems into logical workstreams.
- **Data Synthesis**: Write SQL to extract insights from raw client data, compiling results into clean dashboards.
- **Client Management**: Conduct interviews with client operational staff and present findings to project leads and client executives.
- **Research & Strategy**: Conduct market research and competitor benchmarking for growth studies.

## Requirements & Qualifications
- Expected graduation with a Bachelor's degree (Finance, Economics, Business, or Engineering) by Summer 2027.
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with a track record of quantitative leadership.
- Proficiency with SQL, Tableau, and Excel (financial and operational modeling).
- Experience presenting structural findings verbally and in decks.
- Familiarity with modern AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity) as productivity accelerators.
- **Strongly Preferred**: Active portfolio links, personal GitHub repositories, or open-source project contributions demonstrating technical execution.
